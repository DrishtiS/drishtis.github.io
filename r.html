Director - David Yates
Cast - Eddie Redmayne, Katherine Waterston, Alison Sudol, Dan Fogler, Colin Farrell, Ezra Miller
Rating: ★★★☆☆
Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them draws on Harry Potter's rich mythology to deliver a spinoff that dazzles with franchise-building magic all its own. But despite all this; despite the innocence and wonder, despite the heroism and friendship, despite the magic It is Harry. It will always be Harry. Even after all these years.
